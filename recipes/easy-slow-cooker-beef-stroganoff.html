Easy Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff

The following recipe can be found here.


Description

A deliciously rich and creamy beef stroganoff that's easy to prep in a slow cooker with little-to-no fuss. It smells wonderful and is tasty like no other stroganoff you've had before thanks to the addition of cream cheese along with the sour cream. Serve over hot, buttered flat noodles.

Use your trusty slow cooker to make this easy beef stroganoff. The ultimate set-it-and-forget-it-meal, this dinner will quickly become a staple in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon dried chives
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • ½ cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • ½ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Spread beef stew meat into bottom of slow cooker crock; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Layer onion over the beef.
  3. Pour mushroom soup and water over the beef; add chives, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and beef bouillon.
  4. Whisk red wine, cornstarch, and flour together in a small bowl; pour over the mixture in the slow cooker.
  5. Cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  6. Stir sour cream, mushrooms, cream cheese, and parsley into the beef mixture; continue cooking for 1 hour more.
  7. Serve over pasta.
